Vinayagar Chadurthi


Vinayagar song 9 kolum

Ganesha or (Ganesh) is the elephant-headed god in Hindu Religion. He is the son of God Shiva and Goddess Parvati. Ganesha is a very popular god in Hindu Religion, and is one of the most worshipped. Hindu tradition states that Ganesha is a god of wisdom, success and good luck.

Riddhi : Shri Ganesha ji gives riddhi or prosperity to his true devotees ! Thus, devotees worship him before embarking on an important work . Buddhi : Shri Ganesha ji gives buddhi or wisdom to perform good deeds. His blessings can really work wonder in any one's life !
